Wednesday, August 20, 2014

v  City-County Consolidations

o    Found in the “County Structure” category under the “Government Structure” dataset, the “City-County Consolidations” indicator shows which counties in the U.S. were incorporated through the consolidation with a municipality.  In addition, clicking on any of these city-county consolidations reveals detailed information such as the “Year of Consolidation”, “Former Entities”, and the “Source” document, if available online.  This is a unique dataset collected by the National Association of Counties.
